About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate 4th-year student at UC San Diego, immersed in the fascinating world of Human Biology. From a young age, my love for crafting and creating extended to various forms of art, including paintings, culinary delights, and intricate crafts. Little did I know that this passion would evolve into a deep-seated interest in the craftsmanship of dental health.

Teeth, to me, are works of art—precise, intricate, and fundamental to our well-being.

My journey has been enriched by a love for exploration and travel, allowing me to witness the diversity of our world. It's during these experiences that I've come to appreciate the importance of envisioning dental care on a global scale. Basic oral healthcare has the power to transform lives, even in the most distant corners of the world.

As I prepare to embark on the journey to dental school, my goal is to merge the art and practice of dentistry into a powerful force for outreach. I believe in the potential of dentistry to make a positive impact on the lives of others globally. Together, let's work towards a new future for dentistry—one that reaches far beyond clinical walls and creates meaningful change for people around the world.
